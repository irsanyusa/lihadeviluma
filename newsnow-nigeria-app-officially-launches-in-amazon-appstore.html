    NewsNow Nigeria makes it easier for users to filter, read, rate, edit, and respectfully discuss world news and current events, especially while on the go. Users with a NewsNow Nigeria-verified account can share posts to a page dedicated exclusively for those who can see topics from multiple viewpoints on social media.

    NewsNow Nigeria reviews verified account posts through several fact-checking methods. The app offers users the ability to easily filter by category and trending topics among other filter options, making it easy for users to scroll through news discussion based on their personal preferences.

    NewsNow Nigeria content is vetted by a robust user rating system and vetted by NewsNow Nigeria fact checkers at the verified account level. Users can read credible and informative news posts relevant to what they care about. When a post is shared, it can be rated by users as "Chatter" which lowers the users account rating, or can be rated as "Informed Opinion" or "Just Facts," which improves a user's account rating. NewsNow Nigeria is dedicated to upholding a community that holds itself accountable for sharing reliable news posts. NewsNow NIgeria empowers its users to think critically and engage in informed discussions using facts and verified information.
