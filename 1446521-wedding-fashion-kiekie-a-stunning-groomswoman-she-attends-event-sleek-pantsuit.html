    Gone are the days were the groomsmen party had to consist of only men. These days, ladies are stepping into that role with just the right amount of panache and class!

    Nigerian fashionista and content creator, Bukunmi Adeaga-Ilori who is better known as Kiekie, had her own fair share of fun over the weekend as she too - just like many others - attended a wedding.

    However, she didn't just go as yet another guest, but as a groomswoman.

    The fashionista took to her Instagram page to share a series of photos showing off her stylish look at the wedding.

    In the photos, Kiekie is seen rocking a grey suit with a white shirt and a purple print tie, just like the men she strikes a pose with.
