    While performing in Boston this week, Nick Jonas took a fall on stage but got up and continued to sing. Video of the incident was posted on TikTok. The Jonas Brothers are currently performing concerts around the country on The Tour.

    Nick Jonas Falls During Jonas Brothers Concert

    While performing the song “Sail Away” during Tuesday’s concert, Jonas stepped to the edge of the stage. When his back was turned, part of the stage appears to have been lowered, leaving a hole behind him. Jonas stepped backward and fell into it.

    In a video of the incident, a security guard tries to warn Jonas of what’s about to happen, but it was too late. Thankfully, the hole wasn’t very deep, and Jonas was able to quickly climb out of it and rejoin his brothers to continue singing.

    The fan who posted the TikTok video wrote that Jonas “handled it like a champ.” One commenter joked that Joe Jonas got his birthday wish, since the singer’s birthday was on Tuesday. Another wrote, “You can tell Kevin is trying to hold himself from losing it.”

    The Jonas Brothers Are Currently on Tour

    The Jonas Brothers recently embarked on their new concert tour, titled simply The Tour. They kicked things off this past weekend with two sold-out nights at Yankee Stadium. The band has dates lined up around North America through December.

    Next year, the band plans to take the tour overseas, starting in Australia before heading to Europe, with scheduled dates into July 2024. The tour features songs from five of the band’s albums, including their most recent release The Album.

    The brothers released the project in May of this year. The album includes songs such as “Wings,” “Waffle House,” and “Summer Baby.” Hopefully none of the brothers will take any more falls during future performances on this tour.
