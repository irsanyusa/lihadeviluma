    Profession: Composer

    Biography: Salieri was one of the great composers of 18th century and known for operas like "Armida", "La fiera di Venezia" and "Tatare".

    Born: August 18, 1750
    Birthplace: Legnago, Republic of Venice
    Star Sign: Leo

    Died: May 7, 1825 (aged 74)

    Career Highlights

    • 1771-06-02 "Armida", an operatic 'dramma per musica' by Antonio Salieri debuts at the Vienna Burgtheater
    • 1772-01-29 "La Fiera di Venezia (The Fair of Venice)", a commedia per musica by Antonio Salieri first performed at Carnival in Venice
    • 1787-06-08 "Tarare" an opera by Antonio Salieri premieres at the Théâtre de la Porte Saint-Martin in Paris, France
