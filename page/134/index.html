Do White Noise Machines Really Work to Help You Sleep?

24 Jun 2024, 00:00

For those in search of safe, non-drug sleep aids, sound machines are a popular choice. A National Sleep Foundation poll from 2012 found that 5% of Americans sleep with a “sound conditioner,” which is a catchall term for a fan, phone app, or other noise-producing device. Michael Grandner, director of the Sleep and Health Research Program at the University of Arizona, says the “blanket of sound” these create is a lifesaver if you live on a busy street or anywhere else where loud nighttime noise is common.
