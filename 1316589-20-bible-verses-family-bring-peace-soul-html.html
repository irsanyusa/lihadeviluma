    Nothing calms a family down and makes them feel protected like bible verses about family. Believers across the world go to the word of God, which is in the Bible for each of their needs. They believe that the Bible has answers to all their problems, including peace.

    When you know that there is a place you can reach for a word of encouragement then you can rest easy knowing that you are on the right track. Challenges in life can push you to a corner and make you feel like losing or giving up. Whenever a family is grieving, or going through a tough time, more often than not, the following bible verses about family will help them get the peace they need so much.

    Bible verse about family peace

    Every believer knows that the bible is the answer to all their concerns on earth. In times of need, sorrow, tribulation, happiness and daily living, nothing offers guidance and peace better than the word of God. It is, therefore, not surprising that most Christians choose to seek answers to their daily struggles from the holy book. With the various family bible verses, a lot of families have pulled through the toughest situations in life. So, which are some of these go-to scripture about family peace? Well, they are numerous but we are going to focus on 20 of them as shown below.

    1. The peace of Christ

    Colossian 3: 15 reads, Let the peace of Christ rule in your hearts, since as members of one body you were called to peace. And be thankful.

    What is the most comforting Bible verse? Well, there are many of them. The above example is a perfect one. Knowing that you are entitled to peace for Christ sake is enough to embrace it. As a Christian, when you believe that Jesus finished it all on the cross and set you free, you can get through the hardest times knowing that you are entitled to God's peace simply because you have accepted Christ. It is not about your own doing or righteousness, but because of his mercy and infinite love. The verse above will keep your family encouraged amidst all the pain and struggle of the season. Knowing that God is your peace can go a long way in healing any family.

    2. Peace is the fruit of the Holy Spirit

    Galatians 5: 22 reads, But the fruit of the spirit is love, joy, peace, forbearance, kindness, goodness, faithfulness...

    Well, as a believing family that is filled with the Holy Ghost, you are already promised peace as part of the fruits to enjoy. This only means that you can go through any life challenge and storm peacefully. It is not by your own doing, but by the power of the Holy Spirit that dwells within you. While things may not make sense to a lot of people around you, your family will seem to be in perfect peace that no one else can understand. That is the power of the Holy Spirit, and it is perfect.

    3. Do your part

    Hebrew 12: 14 reads, Make every effort to live in peace with everyone and to be holy, without holiness no one will see the Lord.

    This is an urge to all Christians to be peaceful as this is one of the requirements that help people maintain their holiness, which is a prerequisite to seeing the Lord some day. Families need to embrace peace everywhere they are.

    4. Seek and search for it

    1st Peter 3: 11 reads, They must turn from evil and do good; they must seek peace and pursue it.

    Even as you quote scriptures about family, a believing family understands that peace needs to be sought after. As such, they will keep desiring it and doing good for the sake of it. Families that refrain from evil-doing have a lot of peace within their settings.

    5. Leaving everything to God

    1 Peter 5: 7 reads, Cast all your anxieties on him because he cares for you.

    This is one of the simplest verses about family and peace that any household should adopt. Families tend to face a lot of issues on a daily basis. Without the right mindset, some of the challenges that come up every day can be overwhelming and crippling.

    Having to worry about the next meal, a roof over the family's head, the right medication, clothing, and all other basics needed for daily survival can be too much for a family. Fortunately, the word of God is clear about leaving everything for him to handle and make right.

    6. Do not concentrate on revenging

    1st Thessalonians 5: 15 reads, Make sure that nobody pays back wrong for wrong but always strive to do what is good for each other and for everyone else.

    The secret of winning as a family is to let things go. The pressure of having to live in a world where many people are after their own gains are numerous. Your family may suffer injustices as a result of this, and the last thing you want to do is sit back and watch people take advantage. Well, that is exactly what the word of God asks of you. Keep praying for you and your family as you wait on God's vengeance which is sure and perfect. When you do good to others, the peace of God will dwell within you and your family.

    7. He has overcome the world

    John 16: 33 reads, I have said these things to you, that in me you may have peace. In the world you will have tribulation. But take heart; I have overcome the world.

    What Jesus says about peace? Jesus talked about peace in many instances and one of them is as mentioned above. He is the peace you need. In case your family seems to be struggling with peace, they have a way out and it involves accepting Jesus Christ. The world has a lot of temptations but those who are in Christ find peace in the knowledge and belief that he conquered it all. His victory can also be yours as long as you believe and accept him.

    8. The peacemaker reward

    James 3: 18 reads, Peacemakers who sow in peace, reap a harvest of righteousness.

    What is a good scripture for peace? This would be one. Families and believers, in general, are encouraged to be peacemakers for the rewards are immeasurable. It is a great thing to be considered righteous and acceptable before the Lord since we have all fallen short of his glory. Families should be encouraged to choose peace over anything else.

    9. Instruction for peace

    Proverbs 3: 1-35 reads, My son, do not forget my teaching, but let your heart keep my commandments, for length of days and years of life and peace they will add to you. Let not steadfast love and faithfulness forsake you; bind them around your neck; write them on the tablet of your heart. So you will find favor and good success in the sight of God and man. Trust in the Lord with all your heart, and do not lean on your own understanding. ...

    What does the bible say about family one may ask? Well, the scripture has a lot of guidance for family. However, the above scripture captures one of the most crucial instructions on peace for family members, especially for the children. Any family, or individual who wants to succeed in life should rely on God with all their might. Following God's instructions on love and faithfulness will yield the peace that many people yearn for. Families should, therefore, strive to raise generations that abide to this guidelines and they will enjoy the peace of the Lord forever.

    10. The peace of God surpasses all understanding

    Philippians 4: 7 reads, And the peace of God which transcends all understanding will guard your heart and mind in Christ Jesus.

    Every time you choose scripture on family, make sure to include the one above. This is encouraged to all who believe. When Christ is in charge, all who believe get to enjoy his perfect peace.

    11. Peace promoters have a reward

    Proverbs 12: 20 reads, Deceit is in the hearts of those who plot evil, but those who promote peace have joy.

    What does the Bible say about peace breakers? The Bible says that their hearts are filled with deceit. On the contrary, those who choose to promote peace are always joyful for this is the reward from the Lord. If you want to have a happy family, one that has the joy of the Lord, then make peace promotion your priority as a family. Even as you create personal Bible quotes about family for your family, consider putting those that emphasize on peace. Your family's success will be assured if everyone embraces peace.

    12. Do all you can to keep peace

    Romans 12: 18 reads, If it is possible, as far as it depends on you, live at peace with everyone.

    This is one of the most powerful Bible verses about family unity. Peace is a major component for any united family. When people are peaceful, they agree together. Families with discord cannot come together without a fight. The Bible urges all people to work on being peaceful, for their own good.

    13. Peace with the enemies

    Proverbs 16: 7 reads, When the Lord takes pleasure in anyone's ways, he causes their enemies to make peace with them.

    God understands how powerful peace is. When you please him he rewards you with peaceful neighbors. You will be surprised when your enemies turn around just because the Lord's favor is upon your life.

    14. Peace is a blessing from God

    Proverbs 29: 11 reads, The Lord gives strength to his people; the Lord blesses his people with peace.

    15. Strive to attain peace

    Romans 14: 19 reads, Let us therefore make every effort to do what leads to peace and to mutual edification.

    Every family and Christian should make it a life purpose to embrace and encourage peace whenever they can.

    16. Caution to embrace peace

I did not come to bring peace, but a sword. 35 For I have come to turn “ ‘a man against his father, a daughter against her mother, a daughter-in-law against her mother-in-law— 36 a man’s enemies will be the members of his own household.

    17. Pay evil with blessings

    1st Peter 3: 9-11 reads, 9 Do not repay evil with evil or insult with insult. On the contrary, repay evil with blessing, because to this you were called so that you may inherit a blessing. 10 For, “Whoever would love life and see good days must keep their tongue from evil and their lips from deceitful speech. 11 They must turn from evil and do good; they must seek peace and pursue it.

    All Christian families are called to pursue peace by all means. It does not matter if you are provoked to react differently, focus on the end goal for the reward of blessing is assured.

    18. Secret to standing strong

    Psalms 119: 165 reads, Great peace have those who love your law, and nothing can make them stumble.

    Isaiah 9: 6 reads, For to us a child is born, to us a son is given, and the government will be on his shoulders. And he will be called Wonderful Counselor, Mighty God, Everlasting Father, Prince of Peace.

    Christians can rest assured that there belief and trust in Jesus is not in vain. He is the prince of peace, hence has the ability to supply it in abundance.
