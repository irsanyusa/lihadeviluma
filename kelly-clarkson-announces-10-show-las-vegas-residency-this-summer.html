    The Voice coach Kelly Clarkson just shared the news that she’ll be performing a Las Vegas residency later this year to coincide with her upcoming new album Chemistry. The 10 shows will take place in July and August, with tickets on sale Friday.

    Kelly Clarkson Announces ‘Chemistry’ Las Vegas Residency

    On Monday, Clarkson posted about the upcoming Las Vegas residency on Instagram. According to Variety, the show will be called “Chemistry…an Intimate Night With Kelly Clarkson.” She’ll be performing at Bakkt Theater at Planet Hollywood Resort & Casino.

    The residency is being described as “an intimate music experience that spans her two-decade long catalog of award-winning hits.” Clarkson also teased on Instagram that she’ll be singing tracks from her upcoming new album Chemistry.

    “The crowds in Vegas are such an amazing collection of people that simply want to have a great time and that’s what we’re going to do!” Clarkson said in a statement. “So many of my musical idols have had, and still have, incredible residencies on The Strip, and I’m so excited to create my own!”

    Tickets for the shows go on sale this Friday, March 31, at 10 a.m. PT. Presale is available to certain groups, such as Citi cardmembers, as early as Tuesday. Fans can see a list of dates and more information (including details about VIP packages) on Ticketmaster.

    SEE ALSO: KELLY CLARKSON SAYS HER UPCOMING ALBUM COMPRISES EVERY PHASE OF HER DIVORCE

    Clarkson Recently Announced Her Album’s Title

    Clarkson has been teasing her new album for quite some time now, and we finally know the title. Over the weekend, the singer shared that the album is called Chemistry. She said she’s been working on it for almost three years.

    She went on to explain that the album depicts “the arc of an entire relationship,” showing “the good, the bad, and the ugly.” That includes her divorce from ex-husband Brandon Blackstock, which was finalized last year.

    There’s no release date for the album right now, but Clarkson teased that fans will be hearing new music “really soon.” It won’t be long before they’re singing along to the songs as Clarkson performs them at her Vegas shows.
