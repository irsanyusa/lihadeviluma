    Sofie Dossi has gone from America’s Got Talent contortionist sensation to viral TikTok and YouTuber, dancer, singer, and now business owner. This contortionist has seriously gone on to do some great things since her time on AGT.

    After America’s Got Talent, Sofie went on to find great success on social media. Her two biggest platforms are YouTube and TikTok. Sofie keeps her fans up to date with everything she does, including appearances on AGT. Other than that, this contortionist normally posts challenges, vlogs, dares, and even more. Sofie is so big on YouTube that she performed at VidCon in 2017, 2018, and 2019.

    On top of her talents we already know about, she performs multiple hidden talents on her social media. Sofie proves that she’s filled with skill when she sings and dances on TikTok, YouTube, and Instagram. This contortionist is known for creating original dances on YouTube to popular songs like “7 Rings” by Ariana Grande, “FRIENDS” by Marshmello and Anne-Marie, “Bad Boy” by Billie Eilish, and more.

    Since Sofie is such a big influencer now, she’s friends with multiple others, and is even dating one! She’s been dating YouTube and TikTok star, Dom Brack since November 2020. This may come as a surprise to some fans since they kept their relationship private at first. Eventually, they told fans they began dating on Valentine’s day via TikTok. In one of Dom’s recent YouTube videos, the couple confirmed that their relationship started in November, not February.

    Sofie has recently launched a new YouTube original series named “Copy That.” This show brings famous creators together to try and copy each other’s talents. Whether doing makeup, gymnastics, or cooking, this series seems to have it all. The show is now streaming on Sofie’s YouTube channel. On top of her new YouTube original series, this AGT star has recently announced a new line of hoodies and shirts on her website.

    Sofie’s History on America’s Got Talent

    This AGT alum originally took the stage at only 14 years old. 14-year-old Sofie showed up in an all black, skin tight outfit and shocked the judges with her hand balance and contortion abilities. She got four yeses and went on to the judge cuts round where she received a Golden Buzzer from guest judge Reba McEntire.

    Sofie went all the way to the finals before getting eliminated instead of Grace Vanderwaal. To this day Sofie, is known as one of the first aerial acts to make it to the AGT finale. She is also tied with season four finalist Hairo Torres as the contortionist that made it furthest in the competition.

    In 2019, she came back to the AGT stage for America’s Got Talent: The Champions. Sofie’s acts were even bigger and better for AGT: The Champions, but she didn’t make it too far in the series as a whole. She later returned to the stage as a guest performer during the AGT season 14 quarterfinals.
