    11 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Profession: Tennis Player

    Biography: Daniil Medvedev is a Russian professional tennis player best known for winning the 2021 US Open, becoming the world No. 1 in 2022, and winning 20 ATP Tour singles titles, including six Masters titles. He was recognized for defeating the top three players in the world at the 2020 ATP Finals and for his rivalries with Stefanos Tsitsipas and Alexander Zverev.

    Introduced to tennis at age 6, Daniil Medvedev began his junior tennis career in 2009. He won his first junior title in 2010 and reached a career-high junior ranking of world No. 13. His ATP main draw debut came in 2015, and by 2017, Medvedev made his first ATP singles final appearance. His career advanced as he secured his first ATP Tour singles titles in 2018 and reached six consecutive tournament finals in 2019.

    The height of Medvedev's achievements came when he claimed the 2020 ATP Finals title and won his maiden Grand Slam at the US Open in 2021. He also made history by becoming the first person outside the Big Four of men's tennis to achieve the No. 1 ranking since 2004. Throughout his career, he has played notable matches against tennis greats like Novak Djokovic and Rafael Nadal.

    Off the court, Daniil Medvedev is married to Daria Chernyshkova, and they have a daughter. Fluent in Russian, English, and French, Medvedev often credits his improved tennis results to his marriage. An exemption from military service in Russia was granted due to health issues, and he was a fan of FC Bayern Munich.

    Medvedev's presence extended beyond the court with various endorsements, such as partnerships with Lacoste, Tecnifibre, and BMW. His awards and recognition include the Merited Master of Sports of Russia in 2019. He holds records like the most time spent on court at a Grand Slam tournament and the most sets played in a singles major.
